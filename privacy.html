Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Dental Marketing Hub ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our website, services, and tools (collectively, the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. We use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to track your interactions with our site. This includes:

  • Usage Data: We track pages visited, time spent on pages, scroll depth, and traffic source.
  • Identifiable Data: When you submit a form (e.g., to download a guide), we collect your name, email address, and any other information you voluntarily provide.
  • Email Tracking: If you arrive at our site via a cold email, we track whether you opened the email and which links you clicked to attribute your visit correctly.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services.
  • Understand and analyze how users interact with our content.
  • Communicate with you, including sending marketing materials and updates.
  • Nurture leads and tailor our content to your specific needs and challenges.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with third-party service providers to facilitate our Services (e.g., Supabase for database management, Cloudflare for hosting, and Telegram for internal team notifications regarding new leads). We may also share data with our affiliate partners, such as GoHighLevel, if you choose to engage with their services through our links.

4. Your Choices

You can choose not to provide certain information, but this may limit your ability to use certain features of our site. You can clear cookies and local storage in your browser settings at any time. If you wish to opt-out of marketing emails, you can click the "unsubscribe" link provided in those emails.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the forms provided on this website.
