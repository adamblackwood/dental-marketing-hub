Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, and how you interact with our site (pages viewed, time spent). We use first-party cookies and local storage to identify returning visitors and track campaign attribution.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain our website and downloadable resources.
  • Understand how users navigate our site to improve content.
  • Attribute conversions to marketing campaigns (e.g., email campaigns or paid ads).
  • Send you requested resources or newsletters if you opt-in via our forms.

3. Affiliate Links & Sub-IDs

Our site contains affiliate links to GoHighLevel. When you click these links, a unique identifier (Sub-ID) is passed to the third party to attribute any potential sale back to our site. We do not have access to your financial data on third-party platforms.

4. Data Retention & Anti-Bloat Policy

We strictly prohibit data bloat. We do not track every mouse movement or scroll. We only record essential navigational journeys and commercial actions to keep our database lean, ensuring your data is processed efficiently and securely.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via our website forms.
